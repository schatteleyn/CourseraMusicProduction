What is a microphone ?



It is an acoustic-to-electric transducer that converts sound into an electrical signal.

Many types of microphones



But we'll only see two !

Dynamic microphone

Dynamic microphone:

  • Great for the stage, solid and pick-up only what's near.
  • No external power needed.

Condenser microphone

Condenser microphone:

  • Really sensitive, great for the studio
  • Phantom power required (P12, P24, P48)
