Heads Up

In this presentation, I'll show you the three kinds of effects that exists:

  • Dynamic effects
  • Delay effects
  • Filter effects

Dynamic effects

They control the amplitude of the sound.
Don't forget to press the down arrow.

It means, they control the volume based on the material over time.

For examples,

  • Compressors
  • Noise Gates
  • Limiters
  • Expanders

Delay effects

They are related to the propagation of the sound.

They add delays to the sound.

That permit to make the sound feels like it was recorded in a big church, or a little room.

For examples,

  • Delay
  • Reverb
  • Chorus
  • Flanger
  • Phaser

Filter effects

They act on the timbre.

You control the "color" of the sound

For examples,

  • High/Low/Band Pass
  • Parametric EQ
  • Graphic EQ
